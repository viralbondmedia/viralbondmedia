Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2025

Information We Collect

When you register or interact with ViralBond Media, we may collect:

  • Name, Email Address, Phone Number – to communicate and manage your account.
  • Social Media Links & Follower Count – to match you with relevant campaigns and verify audience.
  • Campaign preferences and content – to facilitate collaborations.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services (matching, campaign management).
  • Communicate with you about campaigns, payments, and updates.
  • Analyze trends and enhance platform security.

Data Sharing Policy

We share your data only with:

  • Brands / Influencers with whom you are paired for a campaign (limited to name, social handles, and relevant metrics).
  • Third‑party tools (Google Forms, Google Sheets) that help us collect and organize data securely.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

We never sell your personal data.

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry‑standard security measures (SSL, access controls) to protect your information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

Third-Party Tools

We use Google Forms and Google Sheets to manage registrations and campaign data. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, email viralbondmedia@gmail.com with “Data Request” in the subject line.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to maintain session and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

Contact Email
